About Me

Hello! I'm Caitlin (he/him & she/her), but you may see me online as CDER or CDERTREES. I am a game developer, programmer and artist currently working towards a degree in Digital Game Design.

I have been programming for around 5 years, and doing digital art for even longer than that. Game development is fairly new to me. I've been making games for a just over a year.

Outside of games, I enjoy textile art and electrionics. Recently I have been integrating these interests into my games (see here).
